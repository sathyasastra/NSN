About Me

I am an Assistant Professor (Grade III) in the School of Mechanical Engineering at SASTRA Deemed University, Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu. I completed my Ph.D in Mechanical Engineering in September 2020 under the guidance of Dr. N. Baskar, and hold an M.Tech in Advanced Manufacturing from SASTRA University (CGPA 8.88, Distinction) and a B.E. in Mechanical Engineering from Saranathan College of Engineering (84%, Distinction).

My research spans two interconnected themes: (1) sustainable composite materials — GFRP and natural fibre-reinforced polymers, flame-retardant systems, and additive manufacturing of biopolymers; and (2) machining science — surface-textured cutting tools, nanoparticle-filled lubricant pockets, and multi-objective optimisation of machining parameters using evolutionary algorithms and statistical methods.

My recent work combines these strengths with data-driven modelling: I am developing the GFRP Virtual Tensile Lab, a Streamlit application that uses Gaussian Process Regression and Support Vector Regression — evaluated via Leave-One-Out Cross-Validation — to predict Young's Modulus, UTS, failure strain, and toughness from composite composition inputs.

I am a Life Member of the Indian Society of Systems for Science & Engineering (ISSE – LM04432) and the Indian Welding Society (L01806), and have received two Best Paper Awards at NIT Tiruchirappalli.
